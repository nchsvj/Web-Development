TRANSLATION STUDIES

Welcome to KSA, a premier destination for those passionate about Translation Studies. Our college offers a comprehensive and in-depth educational experience that prepares students to become highly skilled professional translators and interpreters. We focus on developing both linguistic expertise and cultural awareness, which are essential for success in today’s interconnected, multilingual world.

Why Choose Our Translation Studies Program?

At KSA, we believe that translation is more than just converting words from one language to another. It’s about capturing the nuances of meaning, context, and cultural subtleties to ensure effective communication across different languages. Our curriculum is designed to provide a balanced combination of theoretical knowledge and practical training, allowing students to master the art and science of translation.

Key Features of Our Program:

- Specialized Courses: Our courses cover specialized fields such as legal translation, medical translation, technical translation, and literary translation, ensuring that students are prepared for a wide range of professional challenges.

- Real-World Practice: Students have the opportunity to work on real translation projects, gaining practical experience that will help them transition smoothly into professional roles after graduation.

- State-of-the-Art Technology: We provide training in the latest computer-assisted translation (CAT) tools, localization software, and terminology management systems, ensuring that students are proficient in the technology used in the industry today.

- Internships and Partnerships: We partner with translation agencies, corporations, and non-governmental organizations to offer students internships and job placements, providing a pathway to career opportunities.

- Publishing and Media: Translate books, articles, films, and other media to make content accessible to a wider audience.

About the College

The history of the establishment and development of the college is closely intertwined with the history of the city and the country. Since the college’s opening, Vyacheslav Nikolaevich Li, a candidate of technical sciences and associate professor, was appointed as the director. From 2006 to 2020, the institution trained more than 1,000 young professionals across various fields. In October 2019, there was a change in the college’s founding body (as per the founders’ meeting decision on October 10, 2019), and on November 11, 2019, the college was relocated to a new building situated on the left bank of Astana. In April 2020, Gul Sailauovna Zhanaeva was appointed as the general director of the college. Currently, the college operates two campuses (located at 7 Bekturova Street, Zam-Zam Residential Complex, and 29/1 Kunayev Street, Diplomat Residential Complex), both of which comply with sanitary regulations and are equipped with a full range of technical equipment, including an audio alarm system, fire alarm devices, and internal and external surveillance cameras.
